Lead routing & tax (notes from outline)

  • Lead → rep routing: match incoming web leads to a rep by ZIP/territory. Stored under each rep as territory (ZIP list).
  • Multi-jurisdiction sales tax: recommend Shopify Tax / Avalara. CRM stores the rate per state/county and a sales-tax license # per account when provided.
  • Shopify sync: orders, inventory, payments are sourced from Shopify. This UI is the rep-facing layer.
